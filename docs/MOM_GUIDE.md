# Vigil — Mom's Guide

This guide covers everything you need to use the Vigil from your phone.

---

## Your Dashboard

Open the dashboard link Scott sent you in any browser. You'll be asked to sign in with your Google account once — after that it stays logged in.

The dashboard has three sections:
- **Live video feed** — shows what the camera sees right now
- **Controls** — silence button and talk button
- **Recent Activity** — snapshots from the last few hours with the AI's assessment

---

## Understanding Alerts

When the AI thinks Grandma is in an unsafe position, you'll get a **Pushover notification** on your phone. Tap the notification to open the dashboard directly to the relevant photo.

**What the AI looks for:**
- Grandma trying to get out of bed
- Grandma stuck against the bed rails
- Grandma in an uncomfortable or dangerous position

The system checks every 30 seconds, so you'll hear within half a minute of something happening.

---

## Silencing Alerts

If you're already in the room and handling the situation, press **Silence 30 min** to pause alerts for 30 minutes. The badge at the top of the page shows how much silence time is left.

Alerts resume automatically when the silence window ends.

---

## Recent Activity

The **Recent Activity** section shows the last 50 snapshots with the AI's verdict. Each card shows:
- ✓ Safe / ✗ Unsafe
- The AI's confidence level (high / medium / low)
- Whether an alert fired
- A brief reason from the AI
- The time it was taken

Tap any card to open it full-size. From there you can label it:
- **✓ Real Issue** — the AI was right, Grandma needed help
- **✗ False Alarm** — the AI was wrong, Grandma was fine

Labeling helps Scott improve the system over time.

---

## Reporting a Missed Alert

If you noticed Grandma needed help but never got an alert, scroll to the bottom and press **⚠ Report Missed Alert**. This sends Scott a signal that the system may have missed something so he can investigate.

---

## Talking to Grandma

The **🎙 Talk** button opens a two-way audio connection so you can speak to Grandma from anywhere in the house (or remotely).

**This requires the Tailscale app** to be installed on your phone. If the button is grayed out, Tailscale isn't connected — open the Tailscale app and make sure it's running.

---

## If the Video Freezes

The camera feed will reconnect automatically if the connection drops. If it's been frozen for more than a minute, **reload the page** — that's the fastest fix.

---

## Quick Reference

| What you want to do | How |
|---|---|
| Check on Grandma | Open the dashboard |
| Pause alerts while you're in the room | Tap **Silence 30 min** |
| See recent photos | Scroll to Recent Activity |
| Talk to Grandma | Tap **🎙 Talk** (needs Tailscale) |
| Tell Scott the AI missed something | Tap **⚠ Report Missed Alert** |
| Fix a frozen video | Reload the page |
